Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is We Care Sudbury a medical service?

No. We provide non-medical home support only. We do not diagnose, treat, or prescribe. Our services complement — but never replace — licensed medical care.

Do you provide emergency care?

No. For any emergency call 911 immediately. Our caregivers are trained to recognize and report concerns, but we are not an emergency service.

Are caregivers background checked?

Yes. Every caregiver completes a vulnerable sector police check before working with any client. Safety is our top priority.

Can families receive regular updates?

Absolutely. All plans include family updates — weekly on Standard/Premium and monthly on Basic. We contact you immediately if any concern arises.

Do you provide overnight care?

Overnight care may be available depending on staffing. Contact us to discuss your specific needs and we'll find the right solution.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Greater Sudbury and surrounding areas. Contact us if you're unsure — we are actively expanding our coverage.

How do I get started?

Book a free consultation through this website or call us. We'll discuss your loved one's needs and recommend the right plan — no obligation.

Can I change or cancel my plan?

Yes. Contact us to adjust or cancel. We always work with you to find the best solution as needs change.
